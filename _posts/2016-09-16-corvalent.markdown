Corvalent is a Texas-based leader in industrial embedded computing, offering long-life motherboards and systems. At Medialuxe, we worked to strengthen their brand identity and improve e-commerce performance. We designed and developed a new web presence consistent with their high manufacturing standards.

Besides general design and WordPress theme and plugin development, I was also involved in helping create custom product page templates for every motherboard and systems product. These pages needed to be responsive and printable, as well as showcase technical information vital for a sale.
