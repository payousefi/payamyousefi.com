Donna Paula has shot photographs of some of the biggest names in the music industry, including David Bowie, Jimi Hendrix, Led Zeppelin, The Rolling Stones, and more. I built her a photography portfolio showcasing her classic rock photography in an inviting gallery format.

I built an interactive front-end gallery as well as a custom management system for uploading and managing content. I worked closely with Donna in designing her portfolio so her photographs were displayed elegantly. Photo theft was of high importance, and I devised a built-in program to ensure her photographs were watermarked and safe from hot-linking.
